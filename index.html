About

Selfless commitment to the helpless.

Aunty Trust Orphanage and Caregivers, located in Ayama Road, Yenagoa, Bayelsa State, Nigeria, is an
organization that embodies the spirit of compassion and dedication. Despite facing numerous challenges, the
caregivers at this small orphanage work tirelessly to provide a safe and nurturing environment for the children in their care.

Facebook Page

Their selfless commitment to making a difference in the lives of these children is truly inspiring. By offering a helping hand and supporting their efforts, we can join them in their mission to provide basic needs, such as food, clothing, and education, to those who need it most.
